# Project Statement - Fitness Tracker

## Problem Statement

People struggle to record daily workout activities efficiently and consistently. Available mobile applications designed for fitness are either complicated or require an internet connection that can be discouraging. This presents the need for an offline, lightweight, user-friendly alternative to support users in recording workouts, monitoring calories burnt, setting weekly goals, and assessing performance over time.

## Project Scope

The scope of this project includes:

- Command-line fitness tracking system in Python.
- Daily workout information is logged and stored.
- Monitoring weekly progress through calories burned and number of workouts.
- Providing data-based feedback and visualization for better motivation.
- Offering additional utilities, such as calculating BMI or managing workout history.

## Target Users

- Fitness maintenance among students and young adults.
- People who workout at home without fitness applications.
- Beginners looking for a simple and distraction-free workout tracker.
- Users who would like an offline fitness tracking solution with no internet dependency.


## High-Level Features

- Log exercise details (type, duration, calories burned)
- Set and track weekly fitness goals
- Summary of the student's progress on a weekly basis with feedback on goal achievement
- Text-based progress visualization bar chart
- BMI calculator with health category analysis
- Full workout history stored and retrievable
- Deleting wrong workout entries
- Autosave and reload workout data using graphs, charts showing progress over user-defined periods and activity distribution.
